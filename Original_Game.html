I want to start of by saying that I am aware of the turnout of indi game makers, and especially those that build their own game engine. The 1 reason few people are able to make their own game is that necesity to have enduring motivation. That said, daydreaming about video game concepts is my passion. Even though I have no forseeable outcome, I want to at least express my imagination, and create something that didn't exsist before.
Night of the SuperNatural is the code-name/project-name of my game, until a better fitting title is made. It 2 key characteristics are its retro asthetics, 2D platformer RPG gameplay. The game engine would run is though it were simulating a gaming console from the 90's, with the rendering capabilities between a 16-bit and a 64-bit system.

GamePlay

My all time favorite series, ever since I started gaming as a kid, were Mega Man X, Metroid, and Castlevania. I've thrown ideas around of how I could combine the genres. In essence it would the game would be segmented into explorable areas, that contain upgraes/abilites that are blocked by obstacles that require the use of other upgrades/abilities. Generally each area has a path that ends with a boss and an upgrade/ability.
